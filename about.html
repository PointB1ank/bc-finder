About Battle Cup Finder

The battle cup finder was created to simplify the process of finding a party for the Dota 2 weekend battle cup. Currently the only other options are to use the in-game chat, which is a nightmare, or use the reddit thread which still requires you to scroll down the page until you find someone that matches your needs.

How often does the database reset?
Currently the database is wiped every monday at 5pm EST. Adding yourself to the list of eligible players for the next week can be done anytime after this.

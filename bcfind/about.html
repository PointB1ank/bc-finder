About Battle Cup Finder

The battle cup finder was created to simplify the process of finding a party for the Dota 2 weekend battle cup. Being able to search for players that play a certain position in a certain tier has never been so easy. When your group is full, simply remove your post with a click of a button and prepare for battle.

How often does the database reset?
Any un-removed posts will be automatically removed from the database every Sunday so those who want to start looking for a group early won't be mixed in with people from the previous week.

How does it work?
Your profile information: region, tier, steam url, etc. are saved permanently so you can re-list yourself every week with ease. Start by completing your profile, then click "Find a party". Fill out some additional information about your group and click submit. You will be added to the database and automatically appear on the player table.
